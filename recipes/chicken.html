Easy Shredded Mexican Chicken

This flavorful, easy shredded Mexican chicken comes together in 30 minutes, start to finish and is perfect for filling tacos, burritos, taquitos, or loading up on nachos or even into a salad.

  • Chili Powder
  • Paprika
  • Cumin
  • Oregano
  • Oil
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Chicken Broth
  • Chicken
  1. Step 1: Prepare the chicken taco seasoning.
  2. Step 2: Coat the chicken with the seasoning, press it into the chicken with your hands.
  3. Step 3: Cook in a hot skillet with just a small amount of oil to sear both sides. Pour in some chicken broth, cover the skillet with a lid and let the chicken cook until it reaches 165 degrees and is easily shredded.
  4. Step 4: Return the chicken to the pan and stir in the remaining seasoned broth from cooking. This gives more flavor throughout the chicken.
