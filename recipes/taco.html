I used the Costco raw flour tortillas (cut in half), Gordon's fish sticks, this sauce, shredded cheese, guacamole and a healthy spring mix (in that order). First bite of them my husband was in awe!!! The sauce reminds me of legit fish tacos at a mexican restaurant in ensanada! Only difference is I had to use lemon juice (no lime juice in the house). Bravo to this recipe! Bravo!!!

  • 3 tablespoons sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon Sriracha sauce
  1. Step 1 - Mix sour cream, mayonnaise, lime juice, garlic powder, and Sriracha together in a bowl until smooth.
  2. Step 2 - Enjoy!
