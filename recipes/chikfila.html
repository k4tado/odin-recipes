Make your own Chick Fil A Sauce at home! This sauce is a cross between honey mustard and bbq sauce and is perfect for dipping fries or chicken.

  • Mustard
  • Dijon
  • Mayonnaise
  • Honey
  • BBQ Sauce
  • Lemon
  1. Step 1: combine the ingredients, add all ingredients to a bowl and stir or whisk together until well combined.
  2. Step 2: chill, let the sauce rest in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes before using.
